Corporate Profile

Bee Four Inc. is an Alberta corporation headquartered in Alberta and operating across Canada, with an additional business presence in Vancouver, British Columbia.

Bee Four Inc. is 100% Indigenous-owned by a member of the Métis Nation of Alberta, home of its registered head office. With a regular presence in Vancouver, British Columbia, the corporation’s work includes many facets of Indigenous reconciliation, cultural awareness training, regulatory, permitting and compliance contracting, professional learning, digital products, mobile applications, and creative technology.
